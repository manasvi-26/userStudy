Welcome to Round 3 Of Dumb Charades

Play Game

You will be given a set of 5 actions one after the other, performed by human 3d model along with the action description.



You need to rank the action performed by the 4 models from left to right; left being least realistic and right being the most realistic

You can rank the models by dragging and dropping the options in your prefered order of realisticness





You can also Zoom in or Zoom out of the action
